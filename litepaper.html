1. Abstract

Aurelium (AUL ) is a decentralized synthetic asset, developed by Perseion DAO and deployed on the Base network. Its function is to provide a token, AUL, which maintains a soft-peg to the real-time market value of one troy ounce of gold. By leveraging a robust system of DAO-managed liquidity, a native crypto-redemption mechanism, and a clear governance structure through Perseion DAO, Aurelium provides a censorship-resistant, stable, and capital-efficient store of value. This paper outlines the protocol's architecture, its economic model, governance, and strategic roadmap.

2. Introduction

The digital asset landscape is dominated by high volatility, creating a significant barrier to mainstream adoption. While fiat-collateralized stablecoins have addressed this, they reintroduce centralized counterparty risk. Aurelium is designed to solve this trilemma by providing a stable asset that is:

  • Decentralized: Governed by a transparent DAO, not a single corporation.
  • Stable: Pegged to gold, a historically proven store of value.
  • Trust-Minimized: Backed by transparent, on-chain mechanisms and treasury holdings.

Aurelium aims to become the premier on-chain collateral, bridging the timeless stability of gold with the innovation of blockchain technology.

3. Protocol Architecture

The Aurelium protocol is designed for simplicity, security, and reliability.

3.1. The AUL Token

AUL is an ERC-20 token on the Base network. Its total supply is fixed and was minted at genesis, ensuring no future inflationary pressures. The primary utility of AUL is to serve as a high-quality, stable collateral asset for DeFi lending and borrowing protocols.

3.2. Stability and Peg Mechanism

The value of AUL is anchored to the price of gold through two primary mechanisms:

  • Protocol-Owned Liquidity (PoL): Perseion DAO actively manages a treasury to provide deep liquidity for AUL against key assets (like ETH and USDC) on major Decentralized Exchanges. This ensures a stable market and facilitates efficient arbitrage to maintain the peg.
  • Native Redemption for PAXG: As a core security and trust-building feature, the protocol includes a smart contract that allows holders to redeem AUL for an equivalent value in Paxos Gold (PAXG), a fully-regulated and physically-backed gold token. This mechanism, funded by the Perseion DAO treasury, establishes a hard price floor for AUL and provides ultimate confidence in its value.

3.3. Oracle Integration

The protocol utilizes a decentralized oracle network (e.g., Chainlink) to source a tamper-resistant price feed for Gold/USD. This oracle is critical for the redemption mechanism to ensure fair value exchange between AUL and PAXG.

4. Governance by Perseion DAO

The Aurelium protocol is a core product developed and managed by Perseion DAO. The DAO's governance framework is responsible for all strategic decisions regarding the protocol, ensuring its long-term health and alignment with its mission. Key governance responsibilities include:

  • Treasury Management: Overseeing the assets in the treasury, including the PAXG reserves for the redemption mechanism and the funds allocated to the PoL strategy.
  • Parameter Adjustments: Setting and adjusting the fees and daily limits for the PAXG redemption mechanism.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Approving integrations of AUL as collateral in third-party lending protocols.
  • Protocol Upgrades: Authorizing any upgrades or changes to the Aurelium smart contracts to enhance security or functionality.

This structure provides clear accountability and professional management while adhering to the principles of decentralized oversight.

5. Roadmap

Phase 1: Genesis & Bootstrapping (Q4 2025)

  • Deployment of the AUL token contract on the Base network.
  • Establishment of initial DAO-managed liquidity pools for AUL.
  • Activation of the PAXG redemption mechanism with initial treasury funding.

Phase 2: Ecosystem Integration (Q1 2026)

  • Secure the first integration of AUL as a collateral asset in a partner lending protocol.
  • Launch a liquidity incentive program on a major DEX to deepen public liquidity.
  • Expand the treasury's PAXG reserves to support higher redemption volumes.

Phase 3: Protocol Expansion (Q2 2026)

  • Explore integrations with a wider range of DeFi protocols, including derivatives and options markets.
  • Research and develop a potential native minting function for AUL, allowing its creation against other forms of collateral.
  • Investigate multi-chain deployments to expand Aurelium's utility across the broader blockchain ecosystem.

6. Protocol Considerations

Perseion DAO is committed to transparency and robust design. Users and partners should consider the following aspects of the protocol's mechanics:

  • Smart Contract Integrity: The AUL smart contract is designed to be simple and immutable where possible. It has undergone a comprehensive security audit to minimize the risk of vulnerabilities. The audit report is publicly available for review.
  • Market Dynamics: While the redemption mechanism provides a strong price floor, the price of AUL on secondary markets (DEXs) is determined by supply and demand. Minor deviations from the peg are expected and create the arbitrage opportunities that help maintain stability.
  • Oracle Reliability: The protocol's reliance on external price oracles is a critical dependency. We use industry-leading, decentralized oracle solutions to ensure data accuracy and uptime, mitigating the risk of oracle failure or manipulation.
  • Treasury Sufficiency: The effectiveness of the redemption mechanism depends on the PAXG reserves held by the Perseion DAO treasury. The DAO is committed to a conservative management strategy, ensuring that reserves are always sufficient to handle the established daily redemption limits.

7. Conclusion

Aurelium, as a flagship product of Perseion DAO, represents a significant step forward in the evolution of decentralized collateral. By providing a gold-pegged asset with a clear, trust-minimized backing mechanism and professional DAO oversight, AUL offers a compelling alternative to both volatile cryptocurrencies and centralized stablecoins. We believe Aurelium is poised to become a foundational element for a more secure and capital-efficient decentralized financial future.
